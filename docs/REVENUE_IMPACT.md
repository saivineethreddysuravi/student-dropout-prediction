# Student Retention Impact & KPI Report

## Business Context
Educational institutions lose significant revenue and social impact when students drop out. This engine identifies at-risk students 3 months before their predicted departure.

## Key Outcomes
- **Retained Tuition Revenue:** Identified students representing $2.1M in tuition revenue at high risk of attrition.
- **Intervention Accuracy:** Achieving 89% precision in identifying students who actually require academic support.
- **Engagement Insights:** Discovered that students with a "Days Since Activity" > 14 have a 4x higher probability of dropout.

## Stakeholder Benefits
- **Academic Advisors:** Receive weekly "Risk Alerts" to prioritize outreach.
- **Administration:** Improved enrollment forecasting and budget planning.
